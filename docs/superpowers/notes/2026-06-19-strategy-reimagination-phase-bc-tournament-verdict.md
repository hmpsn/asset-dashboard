## Concept essences
- **PULSE** — Strategy is a *story you read*, not a console you operate. System writes the first draft of the operator's opinion; cutting a backing card visibly rewrites the client-facing sentence. Client gets the same paragraph, machinery removed.
- **LEDGER** — Earned-before-proposed P&L. Proof of past results is a first-class column; turns a permission request into a track record. Frames SEO as a money machine.
- **QUEUE** — Fast <60s/item keep/cut/park/send triage of system-drafted cards. Keyboard velocity, shrinking pile, one apply. The buildable baseline + the best curation *engine*.
- **PORTFOLIO** — Lead with the *shape* of the cycle (create N / refresh M / defend K) as an archetype allocation bar; curate within buckets.
- **LENS** — Curate one set; project it to the client feed / meeting view / content work-order / keyword targets.
- **CONTRARIAN** — Kill the standing page; Strategy arrives weekly as a clearable "PR to approve" in the inbox.

Foils to mock alongside the fusion: **PULSE-solo (story-first client)** vs **LEDGER-solo (proof-first client)** to isolate the prose bet vs the proof bet. CONTRARIAN is offered as a **delivery-mode toggle** (page vs arrives-as-PR), not a standalone design. LENS + PORTFOLIO killed as standalones (grafted in).
