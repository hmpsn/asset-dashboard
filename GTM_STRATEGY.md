## Target Customer

### Primary: Small Business Owner with a Webflow Site
- Revenue: $500K–$5M/yr
- Team: 1–15 people
- Has a Webflow site (or agency-built site) that's underperforming in search
- Knows SEO matters but doesn't have time, expertise, or budget for a $3–5K/mo agency
- Currently doing: nothing, sporadic blog posts, or paying for tools they don't understand

### Secondary: Marketing Manager at a Growing Company
- Responsible for web presence but not an SEO specialist
- Needs to report on organic performance to leadership
- Wants actionable recommendations, not raw data dumps

### Anti-Customer (Don't Sell To)
- Enterprise companies with in-house SEO teams
- People looking for cheap backlinks or black-hat tactics
- Agencies (they're the white-label opportunity — Phase 2)

## The Three Pillars (Feature Simplification)

Every feature maps to one of three customer jobs:

### 1. SEE — "What's happening with my site?"
*Answers the question every business owner has but can't easily answer.*

- Site health audits (technical SEO score)
- Google Analytics dashboard (traffic, sources, conversions)
- Search Console data (keywords, clicks, impressions)
- AI-powered insights (plain-English summaries)
- Monthly "what happened" digest

### 2. STRATEGIZE — "What should I do about it?"
*The AI strategist that replaces a $2K/mo consultant.*

- Keyword strategy (mapped to your specific pages)
- Content gap analysis (topics you should be writing about)
- Quick win recommendations (low-effort, high-impact changes)
- Competitor keyword analysis (Premium)
- AI chat advisor (ask anything about your SEO)

### 3. GROW — "Make it happen."
*The execution engine that turns strategy into results.*

- AI-generated content briefs (ready-to-write outlines)
- Full blog post generation
- ROI dashboard (track organic traffic value in dollars)
- SEO change approvals (Premium)
- Strategy & implementation hours (Premium)

## Landing Page Strategy

### Primary Goal
Get the email. Start the free trial. Everything else is secondary.

### Hero Message Framework
- **Headline**: Outcome-focused (traffic growth, not features)
- **Subhead**: How it works (AI + platform, not manual labor)
- **CTA**: Low commitment ("Start Free" not "Buy Now")
- **Proof**: Social proof or metric (even if aspirational at launch)

### Page Structure
1. **Hero** — Headline + CTA + product visual
2. **Problem** — Three pain points they recognize
3. **Solution** — Three pillars (See → Strategize → Grow)
4. **How It Works** — 3 simple steps
5. **Pricing** — Three tiers, Growth highlighted
6. **FAQ** — Objection handling
7. **Final CTA** — Repeat the hero offer

### Objections to Address
- "I don't have time for SEO" → The AI does the strategy. You just approve.
- "I can't afford an agency" → Start free. Growth is $249/mo — less than one freelance blog post.
- "I don't understand SEO" → That's the point. We translate data into plain English.
- "How is this different from Semrush?" → Semrush gives you data. We give you a strategy and execute it.
- "Will this actually work?" → ROI dashboard shows you exactly what your traffic is worth.
